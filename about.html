About MUONS

The official digital media authority of Negombo South International School. Capturing excellence. Creating legacy. Powering modern school media since 2018.

Our Story

MUONS — Media Unit Of Negombo Southernians — was founded in 2018 as the official media body of Negombo South International School. What started as a small photography club has grown into a full-scale digital media unit.

We handle photography, videography, graphic design, social media, live coverage, and digital archiving for every major school event — from sports meets to cultural festivals.

Our motto #Prove #Thrive #Overcome drives everything we do. We don't just capture moments — we preserve the legacy of Negombo South International School.
