About MUONS

The Media Unit of Negombo Southernians represents the digital, creative, and technical backbone of Negombo South International School.

Our Mission

To professionally capture, produce, and preserve school events through photography, videography, journalism, and digital media innovation.

Our Vision

To become a model school media unit recognized for creativity, leadership, discipline, and technical excellence.

What We Do

Photography

Professional coverage of ceremonies, sports and academic events.

Videography

High‑quality video production for official school documentation.

Graphic Design

Creative posters, digital promotions and visual branding.

Journalism

Official reporting, announcements and school media publications.

Organizational Structure

MUONS operates under school administration supervision, led by a Head of Media, Coordinators, and Active Members dedicated to maintaining professional digital standards.
