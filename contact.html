Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about MUONS

How do I join MUONS?

+
Register on our portal using the Register tab on the auth page. Select "MUONS Member" as your role and wait for admin approval. You'll be notified once approved.

Who can access the member portal?

+
Students, MUONS Members, Alumni and Tech Team members can all register and access the portal. Each role has different levels of access to features.

Can I request event photography coverage?

+
Yes! Use the contact form above and select "Event Coverage Request" as the subject. Provide event details and we'll get back to you promptly.

How do I report a website bug?

+
Use the contact form and select "Bug Report" as the subject. Describe the issue in detail including what page it occurred on and what steps to reproduce it.

Where can I find past event photos?

+
Visit our Gallery page for all media. You can also follow our Instagram and Facebook pages where we regularly post event highlights.

Is MUONS only for photography?

+
No! MUONS covers photography, videography, graphic design, social media management, live event coverage, and digital archiving — a full media unit.
